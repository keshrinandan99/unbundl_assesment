Frequently Asked Questions

Can i choose the dentist on my own for treatment ? +

Yes, you can request a specific dentist when booking your appointment. We'll do our best to accommodate your preference based on availability at your chosen clinic.

Will i get the same doctor each time i visit the same clinic ? +

We strive to provide continuity of care. You can mention your preferred dentist while booking and we will try to schedule your appointment with the same doctor whenever possible.

How you are able to provide less prices than other dental brands ? +

Our large network of 600+ clinics allows us to leverage economies of scale, reducing operational costs and passing on the savings directly to our patients without compromising quality.

How you are able to provide less prices than other dental brands ? +

We partner with leading manufacturers and maintain strict quality standards, ensuring you get world-class treatment at affordable prices across all our clinics nationwide.

What are your dental clinic timings? +

Most Clove Dental clinics are open 7 days a week from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Timings may vary slightly by location — you can check your nearest clinic's hours on our website.

How can I book an appointment? +

You can book an appointment online through our website, call our helpline, or visit your nearest Clove Dental clinic directly. Online booking is available 24/7 for your convenience.

How frequently should I visit a dentist? +

It is generally recommended to visit a dentist every 6 months for a routine check-up and cleaning. However, if you have specific dental concerns, more frequent visits may be advised.
